About This Resource

Durham County Civic Hub is a free, nonpartisan public resource maintained as a service to Durham County residents. This site aggregates local news, tracks public meetings, explains the county budget, and links to government services — all in one place.

This site does not produce original reporting. All news links point to their original source. Meeting information is sourced directly from Durham County and Durham Public Schools.

Have a suggestion, correction, or want to contribute? Reach out to Commissioner Allam's office.
