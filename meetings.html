DPS Board of Education

Seven elected board members who govern Durham Public Schools. DPS serves about 32,000 students at 53 schools countywide. The board sets district policy, approves the annual budget, and selects the superintendent.

📅 3rd Thursday of each month · 5:30 PM 📍 511 Cleveland St., Durham

Durham City Council

Durham's seven-member city council governs city operations, sets the city tax rate, and adopts the city budget. The mayor and six council members are elected at large. Meetings are livestreamed and archived on the city website.

📅 1st & 3rd Monday of each month · 7:00 PM 📍 Durham City Hall, 101 City Hall Plaza
