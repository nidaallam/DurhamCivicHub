How to Speak at a Meeting

  • 1Sign up online at dconc.gov or arrive at 200 E. Main St. by 8:45 AM to sign in person.
  • 2Each speaker gets 3 minutes. You may speak on any agenda item or on general county business.
  • 3Written comments submitted before 5:00 PM the day before the meeting are also entered into the record.
  • 4Meetings are broadcast live on Durham County's YouTube channel and website.
