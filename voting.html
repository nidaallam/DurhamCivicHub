About This Site

A Message from Commissioner Nida Allam

I made Durham Civic Hub because finding basic info about your own government shouldn't take hours of googling. Meeting schedules, budget numbers, civic news. It's all here and free for every Durham neighbor.

As Vice Chair of the Durham County Board of Commissioners, I want every Durham neighbor to know who represents them and how to show up. Every link goes directly to an official source. Please reach out anytime!

Not an official government website. Inspired by Apex Town Councilmember Terry Mahaffey's Peak News Network.
